The worlds premiere organization looking into the rights and welfare of political prisoners. Amnesty does great work ensuring that we in more liberal nations know about the struggles of those less fortunate than ourselves for freedom of thought, expression, religion and political persuasion.

Human Rights Watch is dedicated to protecting the human rights of people around the world. They investigate and expose human rights violations and hold abusers accountable. They challenge governments and those who hold power to end abusive practices and respect international human rights law. Visit this site, and check out the annual Human Rights Watch report for the global picture.
